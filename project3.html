Raging Riders

    Our game, Raging Riders, is a 2D endless game where you control a motorbike  traversing across randomly-generated terrain. The idea for this game came about  when the group began discussing classic mobile games from the late-2000s and  early-2010s. We wanted to recreate one particular game, Bike Race, as an homage to  this era of gaming.
